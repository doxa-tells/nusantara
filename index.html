Who We Are

A trusted facilitator between Malaysia and Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan and Central Asia.

Nusantara Kazakhstan LLP is registered in Kazakhstan and headquartered in Almaty. We help foreign businesses, embassies, ministries and educational institutions establish a real presence in Kazakhstan and the surrounding CIS countries — and we open the same door in the opposite direction for Kazakhstani partners reaching into Southeast Asia and Turkey.

Our professional relationships span government ministries, business entities and private sector projects.
